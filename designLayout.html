My original Layout

My original layout consisted of a big blocky blue header, and most of my pages were blue. However, I was not familiar with flex properties therefore my page was not very responsive and not well designed which made me have issues with placing images around my page. I used multiple images and rounded the edges in order to create a more appealing and casual look. However, due to my lack of experience, the pages were not necessarily appealing and were difficult to use due to me only sizing my containers and images with pixels.

Comparison
My current layout

My current layout for my webpages is a very different compared to the previous iterations of my website. I removed the big header and replaced it with a smaller header that includes a button to contact me.I also made all the images responsive and my page is optimized for all sizes. Some of the key considerations included the overall aesthetic and branding of the website, the user experience and navigation, and the functionality and capabilities of the site.
